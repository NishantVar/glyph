## Parameters

- **audience** (Audience): technical background. Default: "engineer".

## Constraints

- **Require:** Use a real source-to-output walkthrough instead of a feature list.
- **Must avoid:** turning the tour into a disconnected syntax checklist.

## Steps

1. If the audience is a beginner:
   a. Follow the walkthrough procedure, pausing to name each construct as it appears.
   Otherwise:
   a. Follow the walkthrough procedure and produce a concise source-to-output walkthrough.

### Procedure: walkthrough

1. Show {audience} one import, constraint, block, branch, with modifier, and return.
